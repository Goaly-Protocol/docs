---
title: "The yield agent"
description: "A bot that keeps the pool earning the most, automatically."
---

To make the prizes as big as possible, Goaly runs a small **robot wallet** — built on Tether's
**WDK** (Wallet Development Kit).

It does one job, over and over, with no human involved:

- **Looks everywhere** — it checks every Morpho vault, on every chain, in USDT and USDC.
- **Moves the money to the best one** — if a safer vault pays more, it moves the pool's funds there
  (swapping USDT ↔ USDC on-chain if needed), keeping every stake worth exactly the same.
- **Signs for itself** — it's a real self-custodial wallet, so it approves its own moves.

The result: the pool always earns close to the best available rate, which means bigger prizes — with
nobody having to babysit it.

<Note>
  You can watch what the agent is doing live at [api.goaly.fun/agent](https://api.goaly.fun/agent).
</Note>
