**Goaly is a football prediction game where you can't lose your bet.**

You stake a dollar stablecoin (**USDT** or **USDC**) on who wins a match:

- **Wrong?** You get your full stake back.
- **Right?** You get your stake back **plus a prize**.

The prize isn't paid by other players — it comes from the **interest your stake earns** while it sits
in the pool. So no one loses their money.
