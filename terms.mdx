## 2. Description of the service

Goaly is a **no-loss football prediction protocol** deployed on **Arbitrum One**. In plain terms:

- You stake **USDT** (on-chain, the token is **USD₮0**) on the outcome of a football match.
- Your staked **principal is always returned to you in full**, whether your prediction is right or
  wrong.
- While a match is live, staked principal sits in an ERC-4626 vault that earns yield through
  [Morpho](https://morpho.org).
- The **prize pool is funded purely by that yield** — not by other players' stakes. Winners split
  the yield; principal is never redistributed.

The Service is a set of **smart contracts** and interfaces that let you interact with them. Goaly
provides software; it does not take custody of your funds, act as a broker or exchange, or hold your
principal on your behalf.

## 7. Not gambling and no loss of principal

Goaly is designed as a **skill-based, no-loss prediction protocol**, not a gambling product:

- Your **staked principal is always redeemable 1:1** and returned to you in full — this is enforced
  on-chain as a solvency invariant.
- A **wrong prediction never costs you your stake**; you only miss that round's yield-funded prize.
- Prizes are funded **exclusively by the yield** earned on pooled principal, never by the loss of
  another player's stake.

Because you cannot lose your principal by predicting, the Service does not involve wagering your
stake against loss. Nonetheless, availability may be restricted where prediction or skill-gaming
activities are regulated or prohibited (see [Eligibility](#3-eligibility)).
