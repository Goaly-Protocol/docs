## Before you start

You need a wallet on **Arbitrum**, a little **USDT** or **USDC** to stake, and some ETH for gas.

## Predict

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick a match">
    Open the [app](https://app.goaly.fun), choose a match, and tap **Home**, **Draw**, or **Away**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stake and confirm">
    Choose **USDT** or **USDC**, type an amount, and confirm in your wallet. Done — you're in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Claim

When the match ends, go to **My predictions** and tap **Claim**.

- You always get your **stake back in full**.
- If you won, your **prize** is added.
- Pick which token you want to receive — **USDT** or **USDC**.

<Tip>
  A wrong prediction never costs you your stake. You only miss out on that round's prize.
</Tip>
