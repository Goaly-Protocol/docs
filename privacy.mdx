_Effective date: **July 1, 2026**_

This Privacy Policy explains how the Goaly team ("Goaly", "we", "us") handles information when you
use the Goaly protocol, the app at [app.goaly.fun](https://app.goaly.fun), and related interfaces
(the "Service"). Goaly is a **non-custodial, on-chain** protocol, so most of what it does happens on
a public blockchain rather than on our servers.

<Note>
  Goaly is designed to collect **as little as possible**. Your keys are derived on your device, and
  we never see them.
</Note>

## What we collect

- **Wallet and account addresses** — your public address is used to read your predictions, balances,
  and to build transactions. It is inherently public on-chain.
- **On-chain activity** — predictions, stakes, claims, and transfers you make are recorded on
  **Arbitrum One** and are **public and permanent** by nature of the blockchain.
- **Minimal off-chain prediction records** — we may cache lightweight records (for example, which
  match and outcome you selected) so the app can display your predictions quickly.
- **Basic analytics and logs** — standard technical data such as IP address, device and browser type,
  and pages viewed, used to keep the Service secure, reliable, and improving.

## What we do not collect

<Warning>
  We **never** collect your **seed phrase, private keys, or signatures used to derive your wallet** —
  these are generated and kept **on your device** and are never transmitted to us.
</Warning>

- **No seed phrase or private keys.** Keys are derived on-device; Goaly cannot access or recover
  them.
- **No KYC / identity documents**, unless required by applicable law. Goaly does not ordinarily
  collect your name, government ID, or similar identity information.

## How we use information

We use the information above to:

- Provide the Service — display your predictions, balances, matches, odds, and standings.
- Build and relay transactions you choose to sign.
- Keep the Service secure, detect abuse, and debug problems.
- Understand aggregate usage to improve the product.

We do **not** sell your personal information.

## Third parties and processors

To operate, the Service relies on third-party providers who may process technical data on our behalf
or independently:

- **RPC and blockchain infrastructure providers** — to read from and broadcast to Arbitrum One.
- **Hosting providers** — to serve the app, documentation, and API.
- **Analytics providers** — to measure aggregate usage and reliability.

On-chain interactions are also visible to the underlying protocols and networks (for example,
**Morpho**, **Tether / USD₮0**, **USDC**, **LayerZero**, and Circle **CCTP**), which are outside our
control.

## On-chain data is public and immutable

Anything recorded on the blockchain — addresses, stakes, predictions, claims, transfers — is
**public, permanent, and cannot be deleted or altered** by Goaly or by you. Anyone can read it, and
it may be indexed by third parties. Please keep this in mind before transacting.

## Cookies and local storage

The app uses your browser's **local storage** and cookies for essential functionality, including:

- The **embedded-wallet cache** — so your derived wallet is available across sessions on your device.
- Your **terms-acceptance** record — so we can remember that you accepted the current version.
- Basic session and preference data.

You can clear this data through your browser, but doing so may remove your embedded-wallet cache and
require you to re-derive access.

## Data retention

- **On-chain data** is retained permanently by the blockchain and cannot be removed.
- **Off-chain records, analytics, and logs** are retained only as long as needed for the purposes
  above, or as required by law, and then deleted or anonymized.

## Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold
off-chain, or to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details
below. Note that we **cannot** modify or delete on-chain data, as it is outside our control.

## Children

The Service is **not intended for anyone under 18**. We do not knowingly collect information from
children. If you believe a minor has used the Service, please contact us.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the **effective date**
at the top of this page. Your continued use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes
acceptance of the updated policy.
