---
title: "How it works"
description: "The simple idea behind no-loss."
---

Goaly never touches the money you stake. Here's the whole idea:

<Steps>
  <Step title="You stake">
    You bet USDT on an outcome.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your stake earns interest">
    While the match is on, your stake sits in a vault that lends it into [Morpho](https://morpho.org) and earns interest — keeping a buffer ready so you can claim any time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Everyone gets their stake back">
    When the match ends, every player reclaims their full stake. This is guaranteed on-chain: the vault only ever earns, so principal is always redeemable **1:1**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The interest becomes the prize">
    The interest earned by the whole vault is the prize. Winners split it — and backing an underdog wins a bigger share.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**That's it.** Your bet earns the prize, so you can only ever **win or break even**.

<Warning>
  "No-loss" protects you from a **wrong prediction**. It can't remove the usual risks of using
  on-chain apps (smart contracts, DeFi).
</Warning>
